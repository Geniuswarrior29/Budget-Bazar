Who We Are

Budget Bazar — Raebareli ka Bharosemand Grocery Store

How We Started

Ek choti si soch se badi shuruwat

Budget Bazar started with a very simple observation: people in Raebareli had to travel to multiple shops or wait long hours to get their daily essentials. There was no reliable, fast, and affordable option for home delivery of groceries in our city.

Our founder — a BSc student juggling studies alongside entrepreneurship — decided to change this. Starting with a physical store at Vijay Nagar, Budget Bazar quickly grew into a full-service delivery operation, bringing Blinkit-style quick commerce to Raebareli.

We launched our website budgetbazar.store to make ordering even easier. Customers can now browse products, track orders, earn loyalty points, and get cashback — all from their phone.
